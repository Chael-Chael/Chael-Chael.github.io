# Welcome to my New Research Blog! 🚀

Hello world! Today I am excited to launch my new research blog. As an avid researcher in **Artificial Intelligence** and **Generative Models**, I've always felt the need for a space to share my thoughts and experimental results in a more informal and rapid way than peer-reviewed papers.

## Why this blog? 

The field of AI is moving at a breakneck speed. While journals and conferences provide the rigorous foundation, many interesting insights and "tribal knowledge" often get lost in the shuffle. My goal with this blog is to:

1. **Share my research journey**: Document the process behind my papers.
2. **Explain complex topics**: Break down technical concepts like *Latent Diffusion Models* or *Post-Training* for a broader audience.
3. **Present mid-way results**: Show cool visualizations and early findings.

- **Rich Formatting**: Bold, italic, and lists.
- **Code Highlighting**:
